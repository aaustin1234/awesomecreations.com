About Me

As a Lead Software Test Engineer at Pathful, formerly Strivven Media, I oversaw the quality assurance and documentation of the company's suite of college and career readiness platforms. I have been working in this role for over three years, with a total of more than twenty years of experience in technology, including software development, manufacturing, operations, and R&D environments.

I am passionate about creating user-friendly and secure solutions that enhance the learning and career development of students and professionals. I also enjoy collaborating with cross-functional teams, designing and implementing best practices, and mentoring junior staff. My goal is to continuously improve my skills and knowledge in the fields of software testing and cybersecurity.
